# LUNORA Client Demonstration Script

Follow this step-by-step walk-through to present the LUNORA Premium E-Commerce platform to stakeholders:

## Step 1: Editorial Catalog Browse
* Open storefront home (`/`).
* Highlight responsive value props strip.
* Click **Shop Now** to navigate to `/shop`.
* Demonstrate filtering by checking the "Tote Bags" sidebar checkbox.
* Search for "Pebble" to display the "Aria Pebble Leather Tote".
* Sort products by price to show sorting responsiveness.

## Step 2: Detail Gallery & Cart operations
* Click on "Lucca Micro Carryall" to open the detail page.
* Hover over the main image to demonstrate hover zoom features.
* Select color and click **Add to Cart**.
* Navigate to `/cart`, modify item quantity, and verify discount limits.

## Step 3: Checkout, Coupon discount, and Payment Success
* Click **Proceed to Checkout**.
* Input address and type coupon code `WELCOME200`.
* Demonstrate the ₹200 discount calculations instantly mapping subtotals.
* Select COD and place order. Renders order success receipt with invoice.
